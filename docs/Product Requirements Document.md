## **Product Requirements Document (PRD)** 

## **Portfolio Website – Shib Chandan Mistry** 

## **1. Product Overview** 

## **1.1 Product Name** 

## **Shib Chandan Portfolio – Systems & Full Stack Engineering Showcase** 

## **1.2 Vision** 

To build a high-impact, technically compelling portfolio website that showcases advanced systems, security, and AI engineering capabilities in a way that is quickly understandable by recruiters while still providing deep technical insights for senior engineers. 

## **1.3 Primary Goal** 

- Secure placements in top product-based companies 

- Demonstrate strong full-stack + systems engineering capability 

## **2. Target Users** 

## **2.1 Primary Users** 

- Recruiters (non-technical, quick scanning) 

- Hiring Managers (semi-technical) 

- Senior Engineers (deep technical evaluation) 

## **2.2 User Behavior** 

- Spend 10–30 seconds initially 

- Look for: 

   - Clear role identity 

   - Strong projects 

   - Real-world impact 

   - Clean UI
